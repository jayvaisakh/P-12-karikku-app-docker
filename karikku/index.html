Our Story · കഥ

A glass of Kerala,
chilled and quiet.

Coco Bliss started on a slow Sunday in Pathanamthitta, when a glass of fresh karikku tasted like the whole afternoon. We wanted to bottle that — the morning palms, the brackish wind off the backwaters, the slow patience of trees that take twenty years to bear.

Every tender coconut is climbed for, picked before noon, and pressed within hours. The shells return to the soil. The water returns to you.

"There is no shortcut for tender coconut. Either you wait for the palm, or you settle for less. We choose to wait." — Manoop, Founder
